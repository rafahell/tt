TUK EXPERIENCE

A bespoke way to experience Dublin city

Winding through the small back streets from the old medieval city to the heart of modern, vibrant Dublin! You get to see the famous city landmarks but also experience real Dublin — the best restaurants, pubs, bars and shopping districts. It’s not only the most fun way to see Dublin but the easiest! We pick you up and drop you off in the city centre.
