FAQ'S

  • How many people can go in a tuk tuk?
    A maximium of four people can go in each tuk tuk.
  • Are the tuk tuks covered from the weather?
    Yes the tuk tuks have full rain covers which can be pulled back when the weather is good and closed when the wather is bad?
  • Is it cold in a tuk tuk?
    With heated and seats and full canvas cover our tuk tuks are pretty cosy even in bad weather but they are not the same as a car so we sdvise that you wear your coat if it’s cold.
  • How long is the tour?
    The regular tour lasts one hour.
  • Can I eat, drink or smoke on while on a Tuk Tuk tour?
    We do not allow eating and drinking on the tuk tuks during a tour for halth and safety reasons.
  • Can I get dropped off at a certain location in the city?
    On a regular tour we begin and finish at the same city centre location (unless in the case of private/ group bookings) so we cannot drop you at alternative locations in the city.
  • Is there group discount offered?
    We can arrang groups discounts. Just contact us at info@tuktoursdublin.com or phone us
  • Do I have to book?
    Booking is not essential but recommended as we cannot guarantee you availability if you just walk up on the day.
